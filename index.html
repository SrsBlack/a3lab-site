How the brain works

Four moves, from scattered to second nature.

01

Ingest

We connect your sources, Drive, Notion, Slack, Gmail, your CRM, call transcripts, into one private store. Read-only, encrypted, yours.

02

Connect

The brain maps how everything relates, which decision led to which outcome, who owns what, what's current versus stale. Not a search box, a model of how your business thinks.

03

Recall

Anyone asks in plain language and gets an answer with its sources attached, every time. No more "ask Sarah", no more digging through three years of threads.

04

Act human approves

Once it knows everything, it does the work: drafts the SOW, updates the CRM, writes the follow-up, flags the risk. You stay in the loop, it just stops being slow.

FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

What can you actually build?

Anything that's structured knowledge work: lead research and enrichment, content pipelines, support triage, reporting, meeting prep, data entry. The second brain is the memory underneath; the workflows are what we build on top of it.

How long does it take?

One-off workflow builds ship in 1 to 5 business days. A Pilot Brain is live on your real data in 2 to 3 weeks. Every build includes testing, documentation and a handover call so you know exactly how it works.

Do we need technical skills to use it?

No. You ask questions in plain language, inside the tools you already use. We handle the setup, and you get clear documentation, a walkthrough and ongoing support.

Is our data safe?

Connections are read-only and encrypted, and the store is private and yours. For teams where privacy is non-negotiable, we deploy fully self-hosted setups (OpenClaw, the Automation Box) where nothing leaves machines you control.

Do we need our own AI subscriptions?

Yes, at least one provider account (Claude, OpenAI or Perplexity). We recommend the right one for your use case on the call and design the system to keep usage costs low.

Can we start small?

That's the recommended path. Most teams start with a single workflow build or a Pilot Brain, see the payback, then grow into the Operating Brain or a retainer. No pressure to commit upfront.
