What we build

Five repeatable agent builds, each shippable on its own.

Each is a self-contained engagement. Most teams start with one, see the result, then stack the next.

  • 01

    Lead enrichment pipelines

    A new lead hits your CRM or form. Perplexity researches the company, role, and recent news. Claude drafts personalised outreach grounded in that research. Zapier logs and sends. Reviewable drafts and human approval where it matters.

    Perplexity + Claude + Zapier Connects to HubSpot, Salesforce, Notion, Sheets Delivery: 1–2 business days
    Learn more →
  • 02

    AI content engines

    You define topics and brand voice. Perplexity researches angles, Claude drafts and formats in your voice, Zapier schedules across LinkedIn, blog, and email. Drafts land in a review queue; the bottleneck moves from blank-page to final-pass.

    Claude + Zapier Connects to Buffer, Notion, WordPress, Mailchimp Delivery: 2–3 business days
    Learn more →
  • 03

    Customer support bots

    Claude reads each ticket, categorises it, auto-responds to repeat questions instantly, and escalates anything complex with a full context summary already written. The agent works on the front line of your inbox, not in front of your customer.

    Claude + Zapier Connects to Zendesk, Intercom, Gmail, Slack Delivery: 2–4 business days
    Learn more →
  • 04

    Website & app automation

    Form submissions become enriched leads in your CRM. Booking requests fire off pre-call research. Site events trigger follow-up sequences. The boring infrastructure between your website and your tools, taken off your team's plate.

    Claude + Zapier + Make Connects to Webflow, Framer, WordPress, Shopify Delivery: 2–3 business days
    Learn more →
  • 05

    OpenClaw setup

    An agent that lives in WhatsApp, Telegram, or Slack and actually does things — reads files, sends emails, runs scripts, browses the web. Self-hosted on your own machine or server. Private by default, configured for your specific workflows.
