A typical consultant build

What we deploy for solo consultants, in three layers

Most solo-consultant engagements stack three automation layers in sequence. Each layer is independently shippable. Most start with the content engine to free up thought-leadership time, then add the rest as the practice grows.

1

Content engine in your voice

An AI agent researches trending topics in your niche, drafts LinkedIn posts and newsletters in your voice and frameworks, then places everything in a review queue. You edit and approve — the blank-page tax is gone, but every published post still passes through you.

2

Pre-call research, automated

Conference attendee lists, LinkedIn connections, and inbound leads get enriched automatically — company size, role, recent news, tech stack, and any signal worth knowing. You walk into every call fully prepped without the manual research tax.

3

Onboarding, systematised

New client intake triggers welcome email, NDA, Calendly link, shared folder, and CRM entry — in a single approved step. The first impression is consistent every time, and your admin time on each new engagement collapses to near-zero.
