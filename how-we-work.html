How we work

From problem to live system, in four steps.

Every engagement runs on the same shape. You tell us the problem. We architect the solution, ship it fast, and stay close as it goes live.

1

Discovery call

Free 30-minute strategy session. We map your current workflows, identify bottlenecks, and pinpoint where automation has the highest ROI for your team. No deck, no pitch, just questions and a sketch of where to start.

2

Solution architecture

We design your custom automation blueprint: which agents handle what, how data flows between Perplexity, Claude, Zapier and your existing tools, where the human-approval boundaries sit, and the expected delivery shape.

3

Build and test

We build, test, and iterate. Most builds ship in 24–72 hours. You get a live demo, full documentation, and a one-hour handover session to walk you through every part of the system before it's yours.

4

Launch and optimise

Go live with confidence. On retainer plans, we monitor performance, fix edge cases, and continuously optimise your agents to handle new scenarios as your business and tools evolve.
