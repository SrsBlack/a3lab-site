How It Works

From ticket to resolution — without the wait

Claude reads, classifies, and responds to common tickets instantly. Complex issues get summarized and escalated with full context — so your team picks up where the bot left off.

1

Ticket comes in

A customer reaches out via email, helpdesk, or live chat. The bot picks it up immediately — no queue, no delay.

2

Claude classifies & answers

The AI reads the ticket, classifies the issue type, checks your knowledge base, and drafts a response for common questions.

3

Complex tickets escalated with context

When the bot can't resolve it, it summarises the issue, attaches relevant history, and routes it to the right team member.

4

Weekly support insights

Get automated summaries of ticket volume, common topics, resolution rates, and sentiment trends — delivered to Slack or email.
