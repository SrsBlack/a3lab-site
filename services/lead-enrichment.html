How It Works

From new lead to personalized outreach — automatically

Every step of the pipeline runs on autopilot. You get qualified, enriched leads delivered to your CRM while you focus on closing deals.

1

New lead captured

A prospect fills your form, lands in your CRM, or is added to a Google Sheet. The pipeline triggers automatically.

2

AI researches the prospect

Perplexity searches their company, role, recent news, funding rounds, and competitive landscape — pulling real-time data with sources.

3

Claude drafts personalized outreach

Claude reads the research, writes a tailored email that references their business context, and formats it in your brand voice.

4

CRM updated, email sent

Zapier logs everything — enriched data to your CRM, outreach email sent (or queued for review), Slack notification delivered.
